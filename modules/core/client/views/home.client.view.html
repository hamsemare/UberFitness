WHAT'S UberFitness?


UberFitness is designed to replace your paper workout journal. UberFitness was developed by two brothers who were disappointed with the selection of overly complicated workout tracking apps, so our solution was a simple, yet visually pleasing, and user-friendly workout logger that is quick and fast to use.



WHY UberFitness?


Other workout apps ????. Here at UberFitness we beieve that a workout tracker should do what just what it says: TRACK! By being simple, we allow users to log quickly and easily, avoiding logging to get in the way of your workout.
